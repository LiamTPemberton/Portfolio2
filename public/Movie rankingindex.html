Liam's best movies

The top 3 movies according to Liam

This is Spinal Tap

Unbelievably funny, and largely improvised script. The most brilliant comedy mockumentary (rockumentary) following the disastrous US Tour of the heavy metal band Spinal Tap

Dead Man's Shoes

Gritty thriller with an awesome twist at the end. Dark and haunting, fantastically acted and scripted.

Lock, Stock and 2 Smoking Barrels

Quotable, over and over again. Brilliant British crime film with great characters and an interwoven storyline.
